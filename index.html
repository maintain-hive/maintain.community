About Maintain

Maintain was a non-profit, volunteer-run community of people interested in maintaining different parts of our world, recognizing the often hidden work done in repair, custodianship, stewardship, tending and caring for the things that matter.

Festival of Maintenance

For a number of years, we ran The Festival of Maintenance, bringing together hundreds of makers and maintainers from a huge range of industries and disciplines:
