We are a community of people interested in how we care for and maintain different parts of our world

{{ page.title }}

Maintain is a community of people interested in maintaining different parts of our world, recognizing the often hidden work done in repair, custodianship, stewardship, tending and caring for the things that matter.

You may know us from our work running The Festival of Maintenance.

We are a non-profit, volunteer-run community, and our festivals are funded by sponsors, including Maker Assembly. Our events have been produced by Newton Halewood Ltd.
